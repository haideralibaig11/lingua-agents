1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Lingua Agents uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By using our Site, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

2. Whats are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device and store certain information about your preferences or past actions.

3. Types of cookies we use

We use the following types of cookies on our sites.

Essential Cookies:These cookies are necessary for the operation for our site. They enable you to navigate the site and users its features.

Performance Cookies:These cookies collect information about how visitors use our site, such as which pages are visited most often. This help us improve the performance of our website.

Functional Cookies:These cookies allow our site to remember choices you make (such as your username, language, or region) and provide enhanced features.

Targeting/Advertising Cookies:These cookies are used to deliver advertisement that are more relevant to you and your interest.

4. How we use cookies

We may use cookies for various purpose, including:

To analyze how people interact with our site

To remember user preference

To personalize content and Ads

To provide social media features

5. Managing cookies preference

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or accept cookies, delete existing cookies, or notify you when a cookie is being sent.

For more information on how to manage cookies in popular browsers, please visit:

Google Chrome: Chrome Help

Mozila Firefox: Firefox Help

Microsoft Edge: Edge Help
